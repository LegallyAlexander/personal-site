About

Hello. My name is Alexander Brooks. I was born in Puyallup, Washington and raised in Auburn, Washington. I'm attending Eastern Washington Univesity to get a Bachelor of Design in Visual Communication Design. A goal of mine is to become an animator for DreamWorks, Pixar, or Disney (in that order). I love designing characters and creating art. I've been drawing since I was ten years old and don't plan on stopping anytime soon.
